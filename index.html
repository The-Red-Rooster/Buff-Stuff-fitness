How does Buff Stuff work? - A general process!

1
Workouts. Our application offers various workouts for users to perform. These activities are simple and easy-to-follow. If users want more in-depth explanations on specific exercises, we provide diagrams or video links to said exercise.
2
Nutrition. Buff Stuff can generate a basic diet plan. The user can follow and adjust their dietary needs as they see fit.
3
Calories. One of the most important aspects to staying in-shape is to count calories. Buff Stuff provides a calorie tracking utility where users can calculate the caloric value of meals to calculate how well they are following their goals.

Caloric tracking and BMI-calculation

Managing caloric intake and maintaining BMI are important steps in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

This is our calorie counting utility. It allows our users to input the foods they eat and the caloric values of said foods and calculates them, allowing individuals to see how they are doing.

BMI or Body-Mass-Index is a measurement of body fat in an individual based on a ratio of height and weight. This calculator will process the height and weight and provide a statistic of the person -- whether they are underweight, normal, overweight, or obese. (WORK-IN-PROGRESS)
